Project PON Berlin

Period: 20 may - 2 june 2024

Introduction

The PON Berlin project's purpose is to have students take part in a 2 week long experience regarding Artificial Intelligence,
during which they'll carry out different tasks using it, improving both technical skills and language knowledge

We didn't like the original experience, so instead we turned it into a more cultural project, visiting different places
and then talking to the other students about them
